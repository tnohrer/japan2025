🇯🇵 Japan Trip Itinerary (Oct 2 - Oct 16, 2025) 🇯🇵

📍 Tokyo (Oct 2 – Oct 7)

💎 Anime, Shopping, Exploring, Mt. Fuji Photo Day Trip

🛬 Oct 2 – Arrival + Night Walk: Land at Haneda (~Evening Arrival), Check-in at Hilton Tokyo (Shinjuku), Explore Kabukicho + Omoide Yokocho

🏔️ Oct 3 – Mt. Fuji Photo Day: Visit Lake Kawaguchi, Chureito Pagoda, Hakone Ropeway

🎌 Oct 4 – Akihabara + Ueno Zoo: Akihabara anime shopping, Ueno Zoo (See capybaras!)

🎨 Oct 5 – Harajuku, Shibuya + TeamLab: Takeshita Street, TeamLab Planets, Shibuya Scramble

🎥 Oct 6 – Ghibli Museum + Nakano Broadway: Ghibli Museum, Nakano Broadway, Gundam Café

🐾 Oct 7 – Capybara Onsen Day: Izu Shaboten Zoo – Soak with Capybaras!, Stay at an Izu Onsen Ryokan

📍 Kyoto (Oct 8 – Oct 11)

💎 Traditional Japan, Shrines, and Relaxation

🎋 Oct 8 – Arashiyama Bamboo Forest: Explore Bamboo Grove, Monkey Park

⛩️ Oct 9 – Fushimi Inari + Nara Deer Park: Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, Feed the bowing deer in Nara

🍵 Oct 10 – Golden Pavilion + Tea Ceremony: Visit Kinkaku-ji, Traditional Kyoto Tea Ceremony, Onsen experience

📍 Osaka (Oct 11 – Oct 16)

💎 Theme Parks, Food, Shopping, Relaxation

🎮 Oct 12 – Universal Studios Japan OR Alternative: Super Nintendo World OR Osaka Food & Culture Tour

🛍️ Oct 13 – Shopping + Capybara Café Osaka: Shinsaibashi, Amerikamura, Capybara Café Osaka

🍣 Oct 14 – Himeji Day Trip OR Osaka Relaxation: Himeji Castle OR Spa World

♨️ Oct 15 – Spa World + Last Shopping: Last-minute shopping, Spa World Onsen

🛫 Oct 16 – Fly Home: Last Tokyo stops (optional), Flight home from Haneda

🛏️ Where to Stay with Hilton Points

🏨 Hilton Tokyo (Shinjuku) (Oct 2-7)

🏨 Izu Onsen Ryokan (Oct 7, paid stay for capybara onsen experience)

🏨 Hilton Kyoto (Oct 8-11)

🏨 Hilton Osaka (Oct 11-16)

💡 Point Strategy: Use Hilton Points in Tokyo & Osaka, Paid stay in Izu, Mix cash & points in Kyoto
